# travel_app

TrekFlow is a cutting-edge travel planning app that enhances your travel experience by utilizing Hive for local database management. Admins can easily add new places with comprehensive descriptions and multiple images, offering users rich content to explore. Users can browse and view all listed places, gaining valuable insights to plan their trips effectively. The app allows travelers to mark their favorite places, write reviews, and share their experiences. Profile management is straightforward, enabling users to update their information effortlessly. With its user-friendly interface and robust features, TrekFlow makes travel planning a seamless and personalized adventure.
